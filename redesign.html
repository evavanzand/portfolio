Redesign travel agency

School assignment: redesign of a website. This was not an assignment from the organization itself.

Inspiration

I started by gathering inspiration. I visited and analyzed the websites of many large travel agencies. My two favorites were Airbnb and Sunweb. To start with, both websites make extensive use of visuals. This immediately puts the user in a holiday mood. In addition, the layout is very clear, the user can easily find what he needs. They both meet all the rules of Ben Shneiderman and the heuristics of Jakob Nielsen. These websites have been a great source of inspiration for me.

Sreenshots from Sunweb and Airbnb. Sources: https://www.airbnb.nl/ -- https://www.sunweb.nl/vakantie

I thought it was important that the site could offer something that I could not find at Sunweb or Airnbnb. In the redesign there are To Do lists. There are specific to do's for each country. When the user logs in he sees this list and can see if he is well prepared for his holiday. There is also a countdown clock. This counts down until the user goes on holiday.

Result

The images below show part of the final result. I chose to keep the colors of the Kloosterreizen logo. Furthermore, I added many visuals so that the user immediately gets into the holiday mood. In addition, I used a pie structure and adjusted the footer. There is still a lot of information in it, but there is now a structure so that it remains clear.
