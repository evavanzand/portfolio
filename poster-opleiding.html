Poster for School

For the Digital Design course, the assignment was to design a poster that promotes your chosen major. In my case, this was Interaction Design. In addition, you had to demonstrably work with Ilustrator and/or Photoshop. Furthermore, you had to come up with a catchy slogan.

Result

The end result can be seen below. I mainly worked with Photoshop. The poster consists of various elements that were added later in Photoshop. To start with, there is the background, with the starry sky, to which I added the rocks myself. The hands are also cut from another image and added to the poster. Furthermore, I made the smoke using a special brush in Photoshop. The font is BEON and seems to give light using the Blurr option in Photoshop. My goal was to give this poster a futuristic theme, because I think that Interaction Design is the future, and specifically my future. Since I have not worked with Photoshop before, I have mainly benefited from the tutorials on YouTube. This assignment was very important to me because it taught me the basics of Photoshop. These basic skills have helped me a lot during the course..
